Frequently Asked Questions

Why are people switching from Sprout Social?

Main reasons:

  • Price: $249+/month is too expensive for many
  • Feature mismatch: Paying for enterprise features they don’t use
  • Better alternatives: Newer tools with modern features
  • Multi-platform needs: Sprout lacks focus on TikTok and short-form

What’s the closest alternative to Sprout Social?

Hootsuite is the closest in terms of features and target audience. It offers similar enterprise-level functionality at about 60% lower cost.

Are free alternatives really worth it?

ContHunt’s free tier offers powerful multi-platform analytics that Sprout doesn’t provide at any price. Meta Business Suite is free and covers Facebook/Instagram management. For many users, free options are genuinely sufficient.

Can I migrate my data from Sprout Social?

Most alternatives offer import tools for:

  • Scheduled content
  • Analytics history (limited)
  • Team member settings
  • Reports

Check with each tool for specific migration capabilities.

Will I lose features switching to a cheaper alternative?

You might lose some enterprise features (advanced social listening, dedicated account management, custom security). But most users discover they weren’t using those features anyway.
