# How to Do an SEO Competitor Analysis

Understanding your competition is crucial for SEO success. Here's a complete guide to competitor analysis.

## Identifying Your Competitors

### Primary Competitors
Sites that rank for the same keywords you want to target.

### Secondary Competitors
Sites in your industry that may not compete for exact keywords but attract similar audiences.

## What to Analyze

### 1. Keyword Strategy
- What keywords are they ranking for?
- What content drives their traffic?
- Where are their keyword gaps?

### 2. Content Analysis
- What type of content do they produce?
- How often do they publish?
- What formats perform best?

### 3. Backlink Profile
- Who links to them?
- What types of sites link of their content?
- Are there link-building opportunities?

### 4. Technical SEO
- Site speed and performance
- Mobile optimization
- Crawlability and structure

## Tools for Competitor Analysis

Use tools like:
- SEMrush
- Ahrefs
- Moz
- SimilarWeb

## Creating Your Action Plan

After analysis, identify:
- Opportunities they're missing
- Content gaps you can fill
- Keywords to target
- Link-building strategies to pursue
