## Cross-Platform Content Strategy

### The Hub-and-Spoke Model

**Hub (Long-form):**
- YouTube video
- Blog post
- Podcast episode
- LinkedIn article

**Spokes (Short-form):**
- YouTube Shorts snippets
- TikTok adaptations
- Instagram Reels versions
- Twitter threads

**Benefits:**
- Create once, distribute everywhere
- Adapt for platform nuances
- Drive traffic to hub content
- Maximize ROI on creation

### Content Adaptation Framework

**Start with Core Concept:**
Example: "5 productivity tips for remote workers"

**Platform Adaptations:**

**YouTube Shorts (60 seconds):**
- Hook: "Productivity hack that changed my life"
- Quick tips 1-2 rapid fire
- CTA: "Watch full video on my channel"

**TikTok (15-60 seconds):**
- Trending sound
- Text overlay with tips
- Fast-paced editing
- Caption: "Save for later"

**Instagram Reels (30-60 seconds):**
- Aesthetic visuals
- Text on screen
- Multiple tips in carousel format
- Engaging caption with more detail

**LinkedIn (Image/Post):**
- Professional tone
- Detailed explanation
- Industry-specific context
- Discussion prompt

**Twitter/X:**
- Thread format
- Each tip = separate tweet
- Engaging final tweet with question
