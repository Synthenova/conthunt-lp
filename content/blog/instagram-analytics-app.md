No. Web apps like ContHunt work perfectly in mobile browsers without downloading anything. They offer the same functionality as downloaded apps with more flexibility.

### What's the best Instagram analytics app for Reels?

For Reels-specific analytics, ContHunt provides the most detailed insights including retention curves and cross-platform Reels comparison. Native Instagram Insights shows basic Reels metrics.

### Are Instagram analytics apps safe?

Reputable apps use official Instagram APIs and don't require your password. Avoid any app asking for your login credentials—legitimate apps connect through OAuth authentication.
