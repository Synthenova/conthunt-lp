**Platform Preferences**: YouTube's algorithm prioritizes retention and engagement over creator identity. Faceless formats that hook viewers in the first second often outperform personality-driven content.

**Evergreen Potential**: Faceless educational content continues driving views months or years after publication, unlike trending personality content that dates quickly.

To maximize your faceless Shorts performance:
