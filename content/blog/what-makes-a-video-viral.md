## 1. The "Vibration" Metric
In 2026, the Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube algorithms all use a variation of the **Vibration Score**. This score measures:
*   **Instant Hook (0.0 - 0.5s):** Does the user stop scrolling immediately?
*   **Emotional Resonance:** Does the AI detect an emotional reaction (via comments or re-watches)?
*   **Social Velocity:** How fast is the video being shared outside the platform?

## 2. The Audience Retention Paradox
The biggest mistake creators make in 2026 is trying to please everyone.

## 4. Engineering the Share
Likes are cheap. Shares are premium. To go viral in 2026, your video must be "Saveable" or "Sendable."
