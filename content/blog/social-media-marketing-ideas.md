## Campaign Ideas You Can Launch

### Campaign Type 1: The Daily Series

**Concept**: Post daily content on a specific theme for 30 days

**Examples**:
- 30 Days of Tips
- 30 Days of Transformations
- 30 Days of Behind-the-Scenes

**Why It Works**: Builds habit for your audience and signals consistency to algorithms

### Campaign Type 2: The Challenge

**Concept**: Invite audience to participate in a shared activity

**Examples**:
- 7-Day Challenge
- 30-Day Challenge
- Weekly Challenge

**Why It Works**: Creates UGC and builds community

### Campaign Type 3: The Countdown

**Concept**: Build anticipation for a launch or event

**Examples**:
- 10 Days to Launch
- Daily Feature Reveals
- Behind-the-Scenes Buildup

**Why It Works**: Creates urgency and excitement

### Campaign Type 4: The Takeover

**Concept**: Let others run your account temporarily

**Examples**:
- Employee takeovers
- Influencer collaborations
- Customer spotlights

**Why It Works**: Fresh content and cross-pollination of audiences

## Trend-Jacking Strategy

Trend-jacking is adapting trending content to your brand. Here's how to do it right:

### Step 1: Identify Trends Early

Use [ContHunt's trend tracking](https://conthunt.app) to spot emerging trends before they're oversaturated.

### Step 2: Filter for Relevance

Not every trend fits your brand. Ask:
- Can I make this authentically relate to my niche?
- Will my audience understand the reference?
- Does this align with my brand values?

### Step 3: Add Your Twist

Don't just copy the trend. Improve it by:
- Adding unexpected elements
- Relating it to your specific audience
- Elevating the production quality
- Combining multiple trends

### Step 4: Move Fast

Trends have short lifespans. Within 24-48 hours of identifying a trend, create and post your version.

## Analytics: What to Track

Track these metrics to evaluate your marketing ideas:

### Engagement Metrics

- **Completion rate**: Are people watching to the end?
- **Engagement rate**: Likes, comments, shares relative to views
- **Save rate**: Is content worth revisiting?
- **Share rate**: Is content worth sharing?

### Growth Metrics

- **Follower growth**: Net gain over time
- **Reach vs. impressions**: How many unique people see your content
- **Profile visits**: Are people checking you out?

### Conversion Metrics

- **Click-through rate**: Are people taking action?
- **Lead generation**: Capturing interest
- **Sales attribution**: Content leading to revenue

## Common Marketing Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Posting Without Strategy

Random posting rarely works. Plan content around:
- Your goals
- Your audience's needs
- Platform algorithms

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Platform Nuances

Each platform is different:
- TikTok: Native, authentic, trending
- Instagram: Aesthetic, curated, lifestyle
- YouTube: Value-driven, searchable

### Mistake 3: Faking Engagement

Buying followers or likes backfires. Focus on:
- Authentic engagement
- Real community building
- Value-first content

### Mistake 4: Inconsistent Posting

Algorithms reward consistency. Establish:
- Posting schedule
- Content themes
- Brand voice

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Analytics

If you don't track, you can't improve. Always:
- Review performance weekly
- Test variations
- Double down on what works

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I post on social media?

For growth, aim for:
- YouTube Shorts: 2-3 daily
- TikTok: 1-3 daily
- Instagram: 1-2 daily

Quality matters more than quantity. Don't sacrifice content for volume.

### What's the best time to post on social media?

Best times vary by audience and platform. Generally:
- Weekdays: 12-3pm, 6-9pm in audience's timezone
- Weekends: 10am-1pm, 7-10pm

Use your specific analytics to find your best times.

### How do I come up with social media content ideas?

Idea sources:
- Your audience's questions
- Trending topics in your niche
- Competitor content (with your unique spin)
- Keyword research
- Your own expertise and experiences

### What type of social media content works best?

Video content, especially short-form, performs best across platforms in 2026. Focus on:
- Educational value
- Entertainment
- Authenticity
- Visual appeal
