# How the TikTok Algorithm Works in 2026

Understanding the TikTok algorithm is essential for maximizing your content's reach. Here's everything you need to know.

## The Core Algorithm Factors

### 1. User Interactions
TikTok tracks how users interact with content:
- Videos watched fully
- Videos liked and commented on
- Videos shared
- Videos saved or bookmarked
- Accounts followed

### 2. Video Information
Each video is analyzed for:
- Captions and text
- Sounds and music used
- Effects and filters
- Hashtags and trending topics

### 3. Device and Account Settings
The algorithm also considers:
- Language preferences
- Location settings
- Content type preferences

## Optimizing for the Algorithm

### Create Engaging Openings
The first few seconds matter most. Hook viewers immediately to increase completion rates.

### Use Trending Formats
Trending sounds and effects give your content a boost in the algorithm.

### Encourage Engagement
Ask questions, create polls, and prompt comments to increase interaction.

### Post Consistently
Regular posting signals account activity and can improve algorithmic favor.

## Understanding the For You Page

The FYP shows users a curated feed based on their interests and behaviors. Your goal is to earn spots on more FYP feeds by creating engaging, relevant content.
